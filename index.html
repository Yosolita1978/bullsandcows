How to play?
  1. Enter a 4-digit number using 4 different digits (0-9, no duplicates) in the input box.
  2. Click "Submit" to guess the secret number.
  3. You'll get feedback: "bulls" for correct digits in the right place and "cows" for correct digits in the wrong place.
  4. You have 10 attempts to guess the number.
